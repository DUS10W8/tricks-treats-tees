Sponsor a Trick-or-Treat Golf Cart

🚗 We have 6 golf carts available for sponsors — just bring your decorations! If carts are booked, you’re welcome to bring your own golf cart instead. Power is available via generator for lights or displays.

Local businesses can host a decorated cart, hand out candy, and (optionally) run a fun mini-game. We provide placement and promotion; you bring the spooky vibes!

What you’ll do
  • Bring your decorations (we provide carts unless you’re bringing your own).
  • Provide sealed, store-bought candy for trick-or-treaters.
  • Wear family-friendly costumes for your staff at the cart.
  • Offer a simple kid-friendly game for guests (see ideas below).
Kid-friendly game ideas
  • Ring toss on pumpkin “stems”
  • One-shot mini putt to “ring the pumpkin”
  • Beanbag/soft “eyeball” toss into cauldrons
  • Glow-ring toss or hoop-the-bat
  • “Guess the candy count” jar
  • Pumpkin bowling with soft pins
