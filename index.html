Sponsor a Trick-or-Treat Golf Cart

Local businesses can host a decorated cart, hand out candy, and (optionally) run a fun mini-game. We provide placement and promotion; you bring the spooky vibes!

What you’ll do
  • Bring a decorated golf cart (lights encouraged) — we can help place you.
  • Provide sealed, store-bought candy for trick-or-treaters.
  • Wear costumes (family-friendly) for your staff at the cart.
  • Offer a simple game for kids/guests (see ideas below).
Game ideas
  • Prize wheel (chance spin)
  • 1-shot mini putt / ring the pumpkin
  • Beanbag toss into cauldrons
  • Glow-ring toss
  • "Guess the candy count" jar
  • Mini basketball / beer-pong style cup toss (non-alcoholic prizes)

Keep it safe, simple, and quick so the line moves. We can help tailor for all-ages.
